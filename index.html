FAQ

Common questions.

What's the difference between the app and 1:1 coaching?

The app gives you the VOLTR method as structured programs you follow on your own. Coaching means I build your program individually, review your data every week and adjust as your life changes. If you want a system, take the app. If you want a coach, apply.

Why does coaching run in 3-month cycles?

Because that's the honest minimum for measurable change. Every cycle ends with a review: progress on the table, next block planned. You decide cycle by cycle, not month by month.

Is there a trial for coaching?

No. The app has a free trial, coaching starts with a conversation. That's deliberate: coaching works through commitment on both sides.

Do I need a full gym?

The app programs are gym-focused: barbells, dumbbells, standard equipment. In 1:1 coaching, your program is built around whatever you have, including home and travel setups.

How do the app subscription and cancellation work?

€49.99 per month, 7-day free trial, all programs included. Cancel in the app anytime, no notice period, no fine print.

What is RPE?

Rate of Perceived Exertion, a 1-10 scale for how hard a set was. RPE 8 means two reps left in the tank. It's how the system adapts the load to your actual day instead of a spreadsheet's assumption.

Am I too much of a beginner for VOLTR?

If you've trained before, RESET is built as your re-entry. If you've never touched a barbell, we honestly recommend a few months of coached basics first. VOLTR is built for people who train seriously, not for first steps.
