The Structure

Four weeks that progressively build volume and load back to baseline. The first week is deliberately easy. The last week leaves you ready for a real program — not broken by it.

Pick your frequency

3x if you're still rebuilding capacity. 4x if you want slightly more stimulus. Both versions move at the same honest pace.

Form focus

Every session starts with movement quality work. Not warm-up fluff — actual pattern re-learning. By week four, the basics feel like basics again.

Built to hand off

RESET is designed to connect directly into BUILD, DRIVE, or HYBRID. Four weeks of re-entry, then you pick where you go next.

Who it's for

This is for you if…

  • You've had a break — weeks or months off training
  • You're coming back from an injury and cleared to lift
  • The last few months were heavy on work or life and training suffered
  • You want to start with structure, not a random week of "getting back into it"
